Smart Home Automation

  • The project utilizes NODEMCU ESP32 as the central controller for a smart home system.
  • DHT11 sensors are used for temperature and humidity sensing, providing data for environmental monitoring.
  • Google Assistant integrated with IFTTT enables voice command functionality for the system.
  • A 5V 4-channel relay module is employed to control various household appliances remotely.
  • The system communicates via Wi-Fi, providing a wide range and eliminating the need for direct line-of-sight communication.
  • Features of the system include individual and group control of lights, temperature and humidity monitoring, and voice control via Google Assistant.
  • Conclusion: Smart home system include reducing physical effort, saving time and energy, and providing convenience and safety benefits for users, particularly the elderly or disabled.
